The TORCH Standard
Required
I. Who We Are
1
TORCH ATL is not a typical studio. It is a private luxury creative estate. Every person inside should feel the difference immediately.
2
Discretion is the foundation. What happens at TORCH stays at TORCH. Artists trust us with vulnerable creative moments. That trust is non-negotiable.
3
Presentation is performance. Immaculate at all times. No fingerprints under direct light. No clutter. The experience speaks before you do.
II. Expectations
4
You are early or you are late. On time is late. Be set up and ready before your shift begins.
5
Shift swaps go through the system only. Informal arrangements between interns are not recognized and will be treated as a no-show.
6
Swaps are track-locked. You may only swap with interns in your same track. Cross-track swaps will be automatically denied.
7
Incidents must be reported same-day. If something goes wrong on your shift, log it through the incident system. Unreported incidents are a breach of protocol.
8
You may not identify yourself as a TORCH employee publicly without explicit written permission.
III. Studio & Facility
9
Studios are reset between every session. Equipment wiped, cables coiled, surfaces clear. Non-negotiable.
10
Guest access is managed strictly. Check the intake list. If a name is not on it, escalate — do not improvise.
11
The address is confidential. Never share, post, or disclose in any format.
12
Performance is reviewed weekly. Your supervisor submits a check-in rating each Friday. These ratings are factored into your end-of-term evaluation and reference eligibility.
